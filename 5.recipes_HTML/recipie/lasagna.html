World's best Lasagna

Descreption:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers wide, flat pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella. This particular recipe, known as the "World's Best Lasagna," is a fan favorite for its hearty and satisfying flavors. It features a robust meat sauce made with sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef, combined with onions, garlic, and a blend of tomato products. The sauce is seasoned with fresh parsley, dried basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and a touch of sugar for balance.

The lasagna is assembled by layering the meat sauce with lasagna noodles, a ricotta cheese mixture (enhanced with an egg for binding), and slices of mozzarella. The dish is topped with grated Parmesan cheese and baked to perfection. The result is a comforting, flavorful meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions

Ingredients:

  • Meat
    • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
    • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • Vegetables
    • 1/2 cup minced onion
    • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Tomato Products:
    • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce
    • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
    • 1/2 cup water
  • Seasonings and Spices:
    • 2 tablespoons white sugar
    • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Pasta
    • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce:
    1. In a Dutch oven, cook 1 pound of sweet Italian sausage, 3/4 pound of lean ground beef, 1/2 cup of minced onion, and 2 cloves of crushed garlic over medium heat until well browned.
    2. Stir in 1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes, 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of tomato sauce, 2 (6 ounce) cans of tomato paste, and 1/2 cup of water.
    3. Season with 2 tablespoons of white sugar, 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves, 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning, 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seeds, 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper, and 1 teaspoon of salt.
    4. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook the Noodles:
    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook 12 lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine 16 ounces of ricotta cheese with 1 egg, 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    2. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    3. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce, overlapping slightly.
    4. Spread with half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
    5. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    6. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese.
    7. Repeat the layers, and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    8. Cover with foil (to prevent sticking, either spray the foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese).
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    1. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
    3. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
